# CML-340 – Product Maturity Strategic Gap Audit

## Overview
This audit evaluates the overall product maturity of **CurManLight** after the post‑push closure of **CML‑339**. It reviews the current state of **PM‑06**, identifies strategic gaps, and proposes the next actionable slice.

## Findings
- **PM‑06** remains at **60 %** maturity (no new runtime changes since CML‑337).
- Documentation and curriculum data are consistent and validated.
- No regressions detected in the runtime pair (`index.html` & `_published_snapshot/netlify-current/index.html`).
- User‑testing evidence from prior slices indicates remaining gaps in onboarding clarity and uniformity.

## Strategic Gaps
1. **Onboarding depth** – limited guidance for first‑time users.
2. **Uniformità** – UX‑07 aspects (icons, terminology) still at early stages (20 %).
3. **Scalabilità del curriculum** – need for structured expansion to support new disciplines.

## Recommendations
- Prioritise a **docs‑only** slice to address onboarding (e.g., `CML‑341 — Onboarding UX Planning`).
- Initiate planning for **PM‑07 Uniformità** to raise UX‑07 progress to ≥ 40 %.
- Prepare a **curriculum expansion** backlog item for upcoming academic years.
